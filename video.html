How to Produce with a Talkbox featuring David Guetta
In an episode of the DJMag Originals, David Guetta showed us from his home how he built a French-house inspired track from scratch using a talkbox in Ableton Live.
Shawn Mendes Reveals his Creative Production Process
In an episode of Pitchfork's Critical Breakthroughs, Shawn Mendes shares his creative process in the studio.
Mike WiLL Made-It Breaks Down How He Produces for Kendrick Lamar & Beyoncé
In an episode of Pitchfork's Critical Breakthroughs, Mike WILL Made-It talks about the approaches he takes towards producing Kendrick Lamar's "DNA", the struggles in working on viral hits such as Miley Cyrus's "We Can't Stop", and the work environment with Beyoncé.
Ellie Goulding Explains Her Voice in the Production Process
In an episode of Pitchfork's Critical Breakthroughs, Ellie Goulding discusses the sounds and lyrics that led to various breakthroughs and her greatest hits.
Third Sons's Exploratory Approach to Music Production
In an episode of the DJMag series 'How I Made', Third Son presents the creative process behind his track "Minicloud" using his eurorack modular.
How Martin Garrix & Dean made 'Used to Love'
In an episode of the Martin Garrix Show, Martin Garrix shows us the studio where he worked with Dean and how one of his greatest hits "Used to Love" came to life working with the team.
How Brooks Broke into the Dance Scene remixing 'Dusk Till Dawn' by ZAYN
At Dancefair 2018, Brooks held a masterclass showing how he produced a remix for ZAYN turning out to be one of his greatest productions: "Dusk Till Dawn".
The Production Value Behind Printwork in London's Nightclubs scene
In an episode of the DJMag Originals, Printworks London shows the nightlife making behind the scenes and how their production work is widely famed as one of the most forward-thinking within global club culture.
Deconstructing the Production Behind Childish Gambino's 'Redbone' with Ludwig Göransson
In this series of Deconstructed by Genius, Ludwig Göransson discusses his eccentric approaches to the collaborative production on Childish Gambino's "Redbone", earning them the Platinum certification.
